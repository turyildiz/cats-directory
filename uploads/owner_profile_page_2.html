About Me

Hi there! I'm Sarah, a lifelong cat lover and certified feline behaviorist. My journey started when I rescued my first tabby, Oliver, back in 2010. Since then, I've dedicated my life to understanding our feline friends better.

I specialize in helping multi-cat households create harmony and reducing anxiety in rescue cats. When I'm not working with clients, you can find me hiking with my adventure cat, Luna, or volunteering at the local shelter.
