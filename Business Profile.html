About Us

At Purrfect Paws Clinic, we believe every cat deserves royal treatment. Founded in 2015, we specialise exclusively in feline care — providing a calm, stress-free environment away from barking dogs. Our team of certified cat specialists is dedicated to ensuring your feline friend lives a long, healthy, happy life.

We offer state-of-the-art diagnostics, dental care, and comfortable boarding suites when you're away. ISFM Gold Cat Friendly Clinic certified.
