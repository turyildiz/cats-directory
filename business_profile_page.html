About Us

At Purrfect Paws Clinic, we believe every cat deserves royal treatment. Founded in 2015, we specialize exclusively in feline care, providing a stress-free environment away from barking dogs. Our team of certified cat specialists is dedicated to ensuring your feline friend lives a long, healthy, and happy life.

We offer state-of-the-art diagnostics, dental care, and comfortable boarding suites for when you're away. Come visit our cat-friendly facility today!
