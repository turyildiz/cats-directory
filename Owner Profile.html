About Me

Hi there! I'm Sarah, a lifelong cat lover and certified feline behaviourist based in London. My journey started when I rescued my first tabby, Oliver, back in 2010. Since then, I've dedicated much of my life to understanding our feline friends better.

I currently share my home with three wonderful cats — Luna, Oliver and Milo — and I also contribute regularly to the Cats.directory blog. I'm passionate about ethical adoption, breed welfare, and helping new cat owners get started on the right paw.
